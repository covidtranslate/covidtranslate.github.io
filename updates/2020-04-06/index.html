Response guidelines for local governments

Update

Today, we're releasing the English version 1.0 of Korea CDC’s COVID-19 playbook for local governments, complete with appendices. This is the amazing work of global volunteers working around the clock since March 28, 2020. This playbook contains detailed, updated protocols for healthcare providers, government officials and ordinary citizens regarding quarantine, treatment, disinfection, funeral arrangements, and more from both a policy and a patient care perspective. We hope making this document available to a broader audience will save lives and help fight this pandemic.
